Savings Planner: Track Your Financial Goals

 Welcome to the Savings Planner, your visual companion on your financial journey towards achieving your goals.

How the Savings Bar Works

 The Savings Bar is a simple yet powerful tool that helps you stay motivated while saving for your financial goals. Here's how it works:

  1. Set a savings goal and specify the target amount you want to achieve.
  2. As you save money over time, update the progress by entering the amount saved.
  3. The Savings Bar will display a progress bar, showing you how close you are to reaching your goal.
  4. Stay committed and watch the progress bar fill up as you get closer to your target.

Add Multiple Savings Goals

 We understand that you might have multiple financial goals. That's why we've made it easy for you to add and track multiple goals simultaneously. Simply click the plus button to add a new savings goal. Each goal will have its own progress bar, helping you visualize your progress towards different aspirations.
